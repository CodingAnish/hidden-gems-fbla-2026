Frequently Asked Questions

Is Hidden Gems free to use?

Yes! Hidden Gems is completely free. You can browse businesses, read reviews, save favorites, and view deals without any cost.

How do I report an incorrect business listing?

Use the help button in the app to report issues. We review all reports and update listings regularly to keep information accurate.

Can I contact the businesses directly?

Each business listing shows their phone number and website. Click through to contact them directly with questions or to book appointments.

How often are deals updated?

Deals are updated regularly as businesses add new offers. Check back frequently to find the latest discounts and special promotions.

How do I write a review?

Find the business you want to review, click the review button, select your star rating (1â€“5), write your comment (minimum 10 characters), and complete the simple verification challenge. Your review will appear immediately to help other customers.

Why do I need to verify I'm human when reviewing?

We use simple verification (solving a basic math problem) to prevent fake reviews and spam. This ensures all reviews come from real customers and maintains community trust.

Why does my match percentage change on the For You page?

Your match percentage is calculated based on the categories and types of businesses you've saved as favorites. As you save more businesses, our algorithm learns your preferences and updates match scores accordingly.

Do deals expire?

Yes! Each deal shows an expiration date. Check the Deals page regularly for the latest offers, and use codes before they expire to claim your discounts.
